An Introduction to Wonderland

WELCOME TO WONDERLAND! HERE YOU'LL MEET ALL KINDS OF CREATURES, GO ON TRANSFORMATIVE ADVENTURES, AND HAVE AN OUT-OF-MIND EXPERIENCE THAT WILL TRANSCEND YOU BEYONF REALITY NTO OUR WORLD OF MAGICAL WONDERS! ENJOY!

ONE FATEFUL DAY, YOU SEE A WHITE RABBIT AND DECIDE TO CHASE IT. IT GOES INTO A HOLE IN A TREE TRUNK, AND YOU FOLLOW IT, FALLING AND FINDING YOURSELF IN A STRANGE NEW LAND. IN FRONT OF YOU IS A TABLE WITH A VARIETY OF POTIONS ON IT…

Choose a potion

Pink, shimmery potion that smells like cupcakes
Clear, bubbling potion that smells like popcorn
Changes color between green and purple potion that smells like wine
Black potion with no smell
Who cares? Drink them all!

INTERESTING CHOICE. THE POTION MAKES YOU SHRINK, AND YOU CAN NOW FIT INTO THE THREE-INCH DOOR IN FRONT OF YOU. ONCE YOU GO THROUGH THE DOOR, YOU WALK ALONG A PATH AND ENCOUNTER TWO TWEEDLES. THEY SAY THEY GO BY TWEEDLE DEE AND TWEEDLE DUM. THEY OFFER TO READ YOU A STORY…
